Case study 01

Private residence, North London

Drone · 360 tour · Cinematic film Shot & delivered in 24 hours
Introduction

One production day. A complete story for the listing.

A family home coming to market with strong competition on the same street. The brief: make buyers feel the property before they ever request a viewing: from the air, through every room, and on the channels where they scroll at night.

The challenge

Listings in the area were leaning on the same flat, wide angle photo sets. Nothing communicated the scale of the plot, the light through the day, or how the rooms connect. Viewings were happening, but the wrong ones, booked by buyers who hadn't really understood the home.

The media strategy

Open from above to claim the location, then move inside without cutting the emotion: a cinematic film for atmosphere, a 360° tour so serious buyers could qualify themselves, and a vertical edit to meet the audience on Instagram and TikTok. One visit, one crew, one kit of files.
