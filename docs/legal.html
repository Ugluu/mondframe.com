Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

Mondframe Ltd ("Mondframe", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. We are the data controller for the information described here.

Who we are

Mondframe Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales. Registered office and company number are available on request and will be listed here once filing is complete. You can contact us about any privacy matter at hello@mondframe.co.uk.

What we collect

  • Contact details you give us through our quote form, by email or phone: name, email address, phone number and the property address relevant to a shoot.
  • Booking and project information: the package chosen, shoot dates, access instructions and any briefing notes you send.
  • Media we produce: photographs, drone footage and virtually staged images of the properties you instruct us on.
  • Technical data collected automatically when you visit the site: IP address, browser type and pages viewed (see the Cookie Policy below).

Why we use it and our legal basis

  • To respond to enquiries and prepare quotes — on the basis of taking steps at your request before entering a contract.
  • To deliver, edit and supply the media you have booked — performance of our contract with you.
  • To send service updates and invoices — performance of our contract.
  • To improve our website and services, and for occasional marketing to existing clients — our legitimate interests, which you can object to at any time.

Property occupants and third parties

When you instruct us on a property, you confirm you have the right to commission media of it and have informed any vendor, landlord or tenant in residence. Where a property is occupied, you are responsible for obtaining the occupant's awareness of the shoot. We will not knowingly capture identifiable people without a lawful basis.

Who we share data with

We do not sell your data. We share it only with service providers who help us operate, such as our form provider (Formspree), scheduling provider (Cal.com), cloud storage and email providers, and any CAA-licensed pilot we sub-contract for a specific flight. All are bound to process data on our instructions only. We may disclose data where required by law.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry data for up to 24 months from last contact. Project records and the media we deliver are kept for up to 6 years to meet tax and contractual obligations, after which they are deleted or anonymised.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict your data, to object to processing, and to data portability. To exercise any right, email hello@mondframe.co.uk. If you are unhappy with how we handle your data you can complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

This policy explains how Mondframe uses cookies and similar technologies, in line with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function and can provide information to the site owner.

Cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary — required for the site to work, such as remembering your form session. These do not need consent.
  • Functional — set by embedded tools such as our Cal.com booking widget when you open it, so the scheduler works correctly.
  • Analytics — if enabled, these help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. We only set these with your consent.

Managing cookies

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies through any consent banner we display, and you can delete or block cookies through your browser settings at any time. Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the site work, such as the booking widget.

Refund Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

We want you to be happy with every shoot. This policy sets out when refunds, re-shoots and fees apply. It sits alongside your rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 where they apply.

If the media is not as agreed

If delivered media does not meet the brief or has a technical fault, tell us within 7 days of delivery. We will re-edit or, where reasonable, re-shoot the affected media at no extra cost. A re-shoot is our first remedy before any refund.

Cancellations and rescheduling

  • Cancel or reschedule with more than 48 hours' notice: no charge.
  • Less than 48 hours' notice: up to 10% of the booking value may be charged to cover reserved time.
  • No access on arrival or a property not ready to shoot: a call-out fee of up to 10% of the booking value may apply.

Weather and airspace

Where weather or airspace restrictions prevent the drone element only, we will deliver the rest of the package and refund or omit the charge for the aerial element. We will offer to return for the aerial footage at the next suitable opportunity.

How refunds are made

Approved refunds are made to the original payment method within 14 days. To request a refund or re-shoot, email hello@mondframe.co.uk with your booking details.
